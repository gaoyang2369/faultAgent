The generator in app.py iterates over `app.state.agent.astream_events(...)` and looks at `event.get("event")` to determine event type.

**The mock_agent.astream_events must return an async iterator yielding dicts like:**
```python

{"event": "on_tool_end", "name": "get_time", "data": {"output": "2026-03-26 12:00:00"}}
```
